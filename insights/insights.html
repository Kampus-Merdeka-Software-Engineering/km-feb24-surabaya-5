Smart Insights

In 2013, profit had a largest increase (9% the previous year) driven by the addition of new categories (Clothing and Accessories),

We found there is high demand in Accessories category, specifically for the product Tires and Tubes, with total sales amounting to 111,412 units.

In the fall season, sales of Tires and Tubes were twice as high compared to other seasons. We might intensify marketing efforts for this product.

There is an anomaly between the Profit Summary and Profit Average, where the SUM profit has been increasing year over year, but the AVG Profit has been experiencing a significant decline.

Although the Accessories category has achieved remarkable sales, the Bikes category remains the largest contributor to profit, despite having fewer sales.
